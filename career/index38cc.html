What Sets Us Apart ?

Corporate Resources has an exceptional combination of people, processes, facilities and track record. Brought together, this enables innovative process development and insightful technology consulting. The typical customer benefits are a faster time to market, increased return on process development investment, and lower risk for our clients.

Corporate Resources has a number of unique characteristics that define us as one of the leading recruitment consultancies in the country.

Non-commission, Salaried Professionals
Our consultants are salaried professionals and are encouraged to work as a team to find the best candidate for your company, as opposed to competing against one another for commissions.

Client Service
We will not compromise on quality in order to secure a commission. Our focus is on finding the right people for our clients with a view to establishing successful long-term relationships.

Training and Development
We invest heavily in training and development to ensure our consultants have the skills to meet our stringent client service KPIs.

Industry Specialists
We employ specialist recruitment consultants for each industry we cover. This ensures we are able to offer our clients expert advice on their specific recruitment requirements.

Accredited Business Processes
At Corporate Resources, Our service levels and processes are regularly reviewed by internal and external auditors to ensure best practice standards.

Candidate Database
We have one of the most extensive databases in India with more than 220,000 pre-screened candidates across the Asia Pacific region. To ensure the quality of our database all candidates are interviewed in person prior to registration.

Managing Candidate Response
Our sophisticated applicant management system ensures that every applicant receives a timely response that represents your company as you would like to represent yourself: professional, personal and polite.

Our people
Our clients tell us that we understand their markets and care about what we do. They tell us we are flexible, open and act with the utmost integrity. Above all they tell us we deliver and are a refreshing change.

Operational excellence
Corporate Resources has a deep understanding of markets and successful transformation. Corporate Resources has a unique track record of running operations. Our combined experiences provide our clients with access to an unrivalled understanding of operational excellence. This understanding helps us to shape and deliver pragmatic solutions providing lasting results.

Sharing risks and rewards
The delivery of results for our clients is our most important goal. We are committed to our clients' success and to helping deliver results that are valuable to them and their customers. That commitment is reinforced by our willingness to share risks and rewards.

The way we work
We work alongside our clients in joint teams and will take responsibility for outcomes. We complement their strengths so as to establish teams with the right mix of skills. Working this way enables us to leave our clients' people with a broader range of skills than when we started.
The strength of Corporate Resources solutions and products comes from our people, our experience, and our partnerships.
At Corporate Resources, we serve our clients by :

  • Partnering with organizations to strengthen leaders and leadership teams to produce superior results
  • Delivering solutions that align leadership, talent, and culture strategically within organizations
  • Employing expert consultants and coaches with deep business experience
  • Leveraging our thought leader partnerships to offer a vast library of leadership content
  • Creating customized leadership solutions designed to target client needs at the individual, team, and organizational levels
