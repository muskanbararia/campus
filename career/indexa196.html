Corporate Resources (CRPL) -Today

Corporate Resources (CRPL) offers permanent, temporary, interim and contract recruitment solutions with international expertise. Our specialized executives with multi sector proficiency are able to deliver comprehensive recruitment process though extensive search and selection, employee assessment and post recruitment facilities be it a single professional to complement your team or an entire work-force. We have the solution to improve the existing capabilities of your organizations
With our commitment to provide exceptional and prompt service, we are the most strategic and inventive part of recruiting for companies and; for career-focused candidates, the channel for perfect synergy between career excellence and an equally rewarding remuneration.
We constantly evaluate our services through market research, innovation and with the latest methodologies, adapting to the ever-changing needs in the industry. We have a proven track record of client-consultant relationship, adding value to the entire process. To further enhance the experience, we provide Key Account Managers with cross functional expertise, onsite recruitment solutions and outplacement schemes.
